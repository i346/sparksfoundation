About Us

Here at Sparks Animal Campus we have cared for many animals that were once housed in a shelter. We know the value of protecting and providing aid for these beautiful animals that are gravely in need of love and support. Many have lost the safety and security of their family and home and are sad and frightened. We urge you to consider donating or helping local animal charities when and if you can. Thank you in advance for your help! The holidays are quickly approaching.
Consider making a donation of money, time, supplies, or adopting a dog from a shelter or rescue program.
